<objective>
Activate the infrastructure layer of the three-layer exemplar plumbing:

1. Add `limits.max_global_exemplars_per_user: 100000` to `mimir-config.yaml` — this is the REAL gate blocking exemplar storage. Mimir's default is 0, which silently discards all ingested exemplars.
2. Add a WHY comment to `otelcol-config.yaml`'s `prometheusremotewrite/mimir` exporter block explaining that exemplar forwarding is unconditional in the PRW v1 translator — NO `send_exemplars: true` key (it does not exist in collector-contrib v0.151.0 and will cause a config parse error).

Purpose: Without the Mimir limits block, `GET /prometheus/api/v1/query_exemplars` always returns `{"data":[]}` regardless of what the SDK sends. The WHY comment is a teaching artifact explaining to workshop attendees why there is no Collector-side config needed.

Output: mimir-config.yaml gains one new top-level `limits:` block. otelcol-config.yaml gains a WHY comment in the PRW exporter section.
</objective>
